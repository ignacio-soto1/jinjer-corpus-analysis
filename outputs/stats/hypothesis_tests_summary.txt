Hypothesis tests · pipeline output v3 (real)
==============================================

This file is a human-readable companion to outputs/hypothesis_tests.csv,
which contains the same numerical results as produced by the analysis
pipeline (process_corpus_discourse_v3.py).

The pipeline tests four hypotheses (H1 through H4). The current manuscript
v19e reports primarily H2 and H4 in simplified form. The repo retains the
full set of pipeline outputs for transparency.


H1 · Progressive delocalization
================================
Test type: Spearman rank correlation between concrete vs metaphorical
           territory density and album release year (n_albums = 7).

Concrete territory density:
  rho = -0.09
  p   = 0.8477
  Conclusion: NOT SUPPORTED (no significant negative trend).

Metaphorical territory density:
  rho = -0.27
  p   = 0.5577
  Conclusion: NOT SUPPORTED.

Pre-registered alpha threshold: 0.10 (exploratory).


H2 · Post-2022 reactivation of displacement vocabulary
=======================================================
Test type: Welch's t-test between Duél (2025) and rest of corpus, on
           per-track displacement vocabulary density (per 100 lemmas).

  Duél mean      = 0.754 per 100 lemmas (n_tracks = 11)
  Rest mean      = 0.227 per 100 lemmas (n_tracks = 50)
  t-statistic    = 0.881
  p-value        = 0.398
  Conclusion: NOT SUPPORTED at the album level (p > 0.10).

Note. The high variance within Duél — one outlier track (Tumbleweed) at
6.58 per 100 lemmas, ten tracks at or near zero — collapses the inferential
test. The track-level outlier becomes the empirical anchor of the analysis;
the album-level null result is reported transparently in §3.2 and §4.2 of
the manuscript.


H3 · Conflict frame transformation
====================================
Tests the diachronic transformation of conflict-frame markers across the
seven albums (Spearman rank correlation with year).

H3a · Passive ratio:
  rho = -0.162, p = 0.7283 — NOT SUPPORTED

H3b · Resistance vocabulary density:
  rho = -0.595, p = 0.1591 — NOT SUPPORTED

H3c · Victimhood vocabulary density:
  rho =  0.126, p = 0.7876 — NOT SUPPORTED

H3d · First-person agent ratio:
  rho = -0.450, p = 0.3104 — NOT SUPPORTED

Pre-registered alpha: 0.10. None of the four sub-tests reaches significance.

Note. The pipeline computes H3 markers but the current manuscript v19e does
not develop H3 as an analytical thread. The H3 outputs are retained in the
repo for transparency and for potential later analysis.


H4 · Gender positional configuration
======================================
Test type: descriptive (no inferential test pre-registered).
Reports cross-album means of feminine and masculine token densities,
relational nouns, social-conventional nouns, and agent/patient ratios.

  Mean total gendered tokens per 100 lemmas       = 1.7716
    Album with minimum: Inhale Do Not Breathe
    Album with maximum: King of Everything

  Mean feminine pronoun density                   = 0.2807
    Album with minimum: Inhale Do Not Breathe
    Album with maximum: Micro EP

  Mean masculine pronoun density                  = 0.4082
    Album with minimum: Inhale Do Not Breathe
    Album with maximum: Duél

  Mean feminine relational density                = 0.0887
    Album with minimum: Inhale Do Not Breathe
    Album with maximum: Micro EP

  Mean masculine relational density               = 0.0929
    Album with minimum: Inhale Do Not Breathe
    Album with maximum: Macro

    Album with minimum: Cloud Factory
    Album with maximum: King of Everything

  Mean feminine-to-masculine ratio                = 0.1570
    Album with minimum: Inhale Do Not Breathe
    Album with maximum: Micro EP

  Mean feminine agent ratio                       = 0.1045
    Album with minimum: Inhale Do Not Breathe

The article reads these distributions in §4.1 through the category of

Reproduction
=============
Run the pipeline against a freshly-reconstructed local corpus:

    python pipeline/process_corpus_discourse_v3.py

Re-running on the same corpus is deterministic.
